Morocco Overview

A curation of the Kingdom’s most soulful corners, from the red walls of Marrakesh to the intellectual heartbeat of Fes.

History & Culture

Morocco is a crossroads of Berber, Arab, and Mediterranean civilizations. Etiquette favors modesty; we recommend covering shoulders and knees when exploring the Medinas.

Cuisine

Expect slow-cooked Tagines, aromatic couscous, and the hospitality of fresh mint tea—the "Berber Whiskey."

Weather: Jan 17–24

Crisp mornings (8°C) transitioning to golden afternoons (19°C). Pack light layers, a warm coat for desert nights, and refined walking shoes.
