History & Etiquette

Morocco is a tapestry of Berber, Arab, and European influences. When visiting mosques or local homes, please ensure shoulders and knees are covered. A small tip (5-10%) is customary for service.

Jan 17–24 Climate

Day: 18°C–20°C | Night: 6°C. The desert cools rapidly at sundown. Layering is essential; bring a light wool coat and comfortable walking shoes for the medina cobblestones.

The Passage

The Sahara Crossing

3 Days · Marrakesh to Fes

Leaving the bustle of Marrakesh, we ascend the High Atlas via the Tizi n'Tichka pass. The journey is a cinematic transition from red clay villages to the lush palms of the Dades Valley.

The Merzouga Night

As the sun dips below the Erg Chebbi dunes, you will arrive at your luxury camp via camelback. Expect a night of traditional Berber music under a canopy of stars untouched by light pollution.

The final leg carries us through the Middle Atlas cedar forests—home to Barbary macaques—before arriving in the intellectual capital of Fes.
